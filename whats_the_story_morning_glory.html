(What's the Story) Morning Glory? (1995)

Release Date: October 2, 1995

" (What's the Story) Morning Glory?" is Oasis's most commercially successful album and a landmark of the Britpop era. Building on the success of their debut, this album delivered a more polished sound with anthemic ballads and singalong choruses that became synonymous with the band. It features their biggest global hits, "Wonderwall" and "Don't Look Back in Anger," alongside other iconic tracks like "Champagne Supernova."

The album's massive popularity cemented Oasis's status as global superstars, leading to sold-out stadium tours and widespread cultural impact. It remains one of the best-selling albums in UK history.
